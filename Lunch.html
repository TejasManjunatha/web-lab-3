HYDERABAD BRIYANI

Hyderabadi Biryani is said to be more spicy and savoury. It is made in the form of Dum and has a lot of Zaffron. Combining basmati rice and chicken makes Hyderabadi biryani distinguishable from others.

RAGI MUDDE

Ragi Mudde are healthy balls made from finger millet flour and water. They are a delicacy and a staple food mainly in Karnataka and in some parts of Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh.
