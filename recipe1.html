MASALA DOSA

Ingredients:

  • First, wash and soak dosa rice in vessel for 3 hours.
  • Same way was urad dal + fenugreek seeds together in another vessel. urad dal
  • After 3 hours, to begin with, drain water from urad dal + fenugreek seeds. Place in the blender.
  • Grind with ice water adding little by little and equally important, in regular intervals to prevent over heating and not all together. In other words, if you grind it right, it will have small bubbles seen in batter. batter with micro bubbles
  • Then, transfer to a large vessel enough to hold both batter. urad dal batter
  • After that, place the rice, salt and cooked rice (if adding) in a mixie (blender)
  • Likewise, using ice water, grind to a smooth batter. Do give time in between if it is hot and also add water carefully without making it watery.
  • Then, transfer it along with ground urad dal batter. mix
  • Eventually, it will take from 12 to15 hours for fermentation.
  • To make dosa, firstly take required portion of batter in a mixing bowl. Then add very little water, say Â¼ cup per 2 cups of batter. preparing dosa batter for masala dosa
  • Mix really well to make dosa. It spreads easily and evenly in dosa pan when we add water to batter and mix well.
